    Who We Are At

    Molly Blunier's Microbakery

    Welcome to our Molly Blunier's Microbakery! My name is Molly, and I'm the passionate baker behind this cozy microbakery. With a lifelong love of all things delicious and bread, my husband, Jake and I founded this small-scale bakery to share my creations with the local community. Every loaf of bread, every bagel, and every treat is lovingly handcrafted using only the finest ingredients and traditional techniques. Thank you for being a part of our community.
